About PALMNEXUS

From palm biomass waste to a traceable carbon economy.

Indonesia produces massive palm biomass waste while industries need credible carbon offsets. PALMNEXUS Carbon+ connects both sides through biochar production, digital traceability, and carbon credit verification.

Problem

Massive palm biomass waste

Palm oil operations produce empty fruit bunches, shells, and fiber. Without a reliable carbon pathway, much of that biomass remains underutilized while companies keep searching for measurable offsets.

Solution

Biochar + carbon credits

PALMNEXUS converts palm biomass into biochar and carbon credits through a traceable digital ecosystem that records feedstock origin, processing data, storage value, and verification status.

♻
Circular Economy
Palm residue becomes valuable biochar instead of unmanaged waste.

🌱
Sustainable Agriculture
Biochar can support soil improvement and long-term land productivity.
